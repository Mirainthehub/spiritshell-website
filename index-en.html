Frequently Asked Questions

How is SpiritShell different from ordinary robots?
SpiritShell combines human teleoperation with AI intelligent systems, offering both human-like genuine interaction and AI's continuous companionship. This dual mode ensures both authenticity and continuity in companionship.
How does the switch between human teleoperation and AI relay work?
The system intelligently determines optimal switching moments and also supports manual control. When human operators go offline, AI seamlessly takes over to ensure uninterrupted companionship service.
Is my data safe and private?
We employ end-to-end encryption technology and strictly comply with data protection regulations. All personal information and interaction data are encrypted and will never be disclosed to third parties.
When will the first units be available?
We plan to begin small-scale pilots in late 2024 and officially launch in 2025. Waitlist users will receive priority access and special discounts.
